# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## Purpose

The purpose of this Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is to establish rules and guidelines for the proper use of the company's information technology (IT) resources. This policy helps protect company data, systems, employees, and customers from security threats and ensures that all users use technology responsibly.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees
- Managers
- Contractors
- Interns
- Temporary staff
- Third-party vendors who access company systems

It covers the use of:

- Company computers and laptops
- Mobile devices
- Email accounts
- Internet access
- Wi-Fi network
- Cloud services
- Business applications
- Storage devices

---

## Acceptable Use

Users are expected to:

- Use company resources only for authorized business purposes.
- Keep usernames and passwords confidential.
- Lock computers when leaving their workstation.
- Report any suspicious emails or activities immediately.
- Use only approved software and applications.
- Store sensitive information securely.
- Follow all cybersecurity policies and procedures.
- Install software only with approval from the IT department.
- Keep devices updated with security patches.
- Use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) whenever available.

---
