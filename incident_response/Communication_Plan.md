# Communication Plan

## Purpose

The Communication Plan defines how information about cybersecurity incidents will be communicated to internal and external stakeholders.

---

# Objectives

- Provide accurate information.
- Reduce confusion.
- Maintain customer trust.
- Ensure timely reporting.

---

# Internal Communication

Notify:

- IT Administrator
- Incident Response Team
- Management
- Employees

Communication Methods:

- Email
- Phone Call
- Microsoft Teams
- Internal Messaging

---

# External Communication

If required, notify:

- Customers
- Vendors
- Internet Service Provider
- Law Enforcement
- Regulatory Authorities

---

# Information to Share

Include:

- Incident type
- Affected systems
- Business impact
- Current status
- Recovery progress
- Expected resolution time

---

# Communication Timeline

| Stage | Responsible Person |
|--------|--------------------|
| Detection | Employee |
| Initial Notification | IT Administrator |
| Management Update | Incident Response Team |
| Customer Notification | Communication Officer |
| Final Report | IT Administrator |

---

# Communication Rules

- Share only verified information.
- Protect confidential information.
- Avoid speculation.
- Maintain professionalism.
- Follow company policies.
