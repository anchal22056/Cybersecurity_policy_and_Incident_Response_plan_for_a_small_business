# Incident Classification

## Purpose

Incident classification helps prioritize incidents based on their impact and urgency.

---

# Severity Levels

| Level | Description | Example | Response Time |
|-------|-------------|----------|---------------|
| Low | Minor security event | Spam email | Within 24 hours |
| Medium | Limited impact | Malware infection | Within 8 hours |
| High | Serious business impact | Data breach | Within 2 hours |
| Critical | Business interruption | Ransomware attack | Immediately |

---

# Incident Categories

## Malware

Examples:

- Virus
- Worm
- Trojan
- Spyware
- Ransomware

---

## Phishing

Examples:

- Fake login page
- Email scam
- Credential theft

---

## Unauthorized Access

Examples:

- Stolen password
- Brute-force attack
- Privilege escalation

---

## Data Breach

Examples:

- Customer information leaked
- Financial records stolen
- Confidential documents exposed

---

## Network Attack

Examples:

- DDoS attack
- Port scanning
- Man-in-the-Middle attack

---

## Insider Threat

Examples:

- Unauthorized data copying
- Intentional data deletion
- Policy violations

---
