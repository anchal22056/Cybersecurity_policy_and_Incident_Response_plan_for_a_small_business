# Employee Cybersecurity Training Guide

## Purpose

The purpose of this Employee Cybersecurity Training Guide is to educate employees about cybersecurity risks, promote safe computing practices, and reduce the likelihood of security incidents caused by human error.

---

# Scope

This training guide applies to:

- Employees
- Managers
- Interns
- Contractors
- Third-party users with access to company systems

---

# Training Objectives

The objectives of this training program are to:

- Increase cybersecurity awareness.
- Prevent phishing attacks.
- Promote strong password practices.
- Protect company information.
- Encourage safe internet usage.
- Reduce insider threats.

---

# Training Topics

## 1. Password Security

Employees should:

- Use passwords with at least 12 characters.
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters.
- Include numbers and special characters.
- Never share passwords.
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA).

---

## 2. Phishing Awareness

Employees should:

- Verify the sender before opening emails.
- Avoid clicking unknown links.
- Never provide passwords through email.
- Report suspicious emails immediately.

---

## 3. Safe Internet Browsing

Employees should:

- Visit trusted websites only.
- Avoid downloading files from unknown sources.
- Check website URLs carefully.
- Use secure HTTPS websites.

---

## 4. Email Security

Employees must:

- Open attachments only from trusted senders.
- Report spam and phishing emails.
- Avoid forwarding suspicious emails.
- Protect confidential information.

---

## 5. Malware Awareness

Employees should recognize:

- Viruses
- Worms
- Trojans
- Spyware
- Ransomware

Report any unusual computer behavior immediately.

---

## 6. Social Engineering

Examples include:

- Phone scams
- Fake technical support
- Tailgating
- Impersonation

Always verify a person's identity before sharing information.

---

## 7. Mobile Device Security

Employees should:

- Lock mobile devices.
- Enable encryption.
- Install updates regularly.
- Report lost or stolen devices immediately.

---

## 8. Data Protection

Employees must:

- Store sensitive information securely.
- Encrypt confidential files.
- Follow company backup procedures.
- Dispose of confidential documents securely.

---

# Security Best Practices

- Lock your computer before leaving your desk.
- Keep software updated.
- Use approved applications only.
- Avoid connecting unknown USB devices.
- Follow company security policies.
- Report incidents immediately.

---

# Training Schedule

| Activity | Frequency |
|----------|-----------|
| Cybersecurity Awareness Training | Every 6 Months |
| Phishing Simulation | Quarterly |
| Password Review | Every 90 Days |
| Security Policy Review | Annually |

---

# Employee Responsibilities

Employees are responsible for:

- Following company security policies.
- Reporting security incidents.
- Protecting company assets.
- Completing mandatory cybersecurity training.

---

# Training Evaluation

Employees should complete:

- Security awareness quiz
- Phishing awareness assessment
- Password security assessment
- Annual cybersecurity training

---

# Review

This training guide should be reviewed annually and updated whenever new cybersecurity threats emerge.
